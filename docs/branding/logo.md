## Usage
- Primary: Emblem above or beside "oro" wordmark (gold center, four-color petals)
- Icon: Petal emblem only, used for favicons, mobile headers, and runtime splash screens
- Monochrome: Solid gold or white emblem when over complex or dark backgrounds

### Clear Space
- Maintain at least the height of the "o" in oro around all sides of the logo
- Minimum size: 32px height (digital) / 15mm (print)
